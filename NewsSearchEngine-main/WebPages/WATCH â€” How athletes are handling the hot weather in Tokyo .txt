Day 2 of the Tokyo 2020 Olympics was jam-packed with events, with swimming finals in multiple events happening throughout the day. Canada’s first medal was won in one of those events, the women’s 4X100m relay final. Swimmers Penny Oleksiak, Maggie Mac Neil, Rebecca Smith and Kayla Sanchez beat the United States to land silver. Australia won gold and set a new world record for the event. 5 young athletes to watch at the Tokyo Olympics But while swimmers could keep cool in the temperature-controlled pools, other athletes were forced to battle with the Tokyo heat and humidity.   They included skateboarders in the men’s street final.
