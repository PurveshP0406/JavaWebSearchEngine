It is Day 1 of the 2020 Tokyo Olympics and the athletes are off to the races, the courts and the ranges. The first medals of the Games were won early Saturday morning with Yang Qian of China taking home gold in the women’s 10-metre rifle competition. And a new Olympic event debuted today. 3X3 basketball tournament pool rounds began for both women and men. And yes, it’s called three EX three, not three ON three. Let’s get into it! Kids News explains 3X3 basketball Watch the latest Kids News Explains with Abigail and Arjun to find out exactly how 3X3 basketball works and the team to watch. 3X3 appeal While 3X3 hasn’t been around as long as full court 5-a-side basketball, it is already a popular international event with professional leagues all over the world. Skateboarding, surfing and other sports debut at Tokyo Olympics It’s a lot like street basketball played on playgrounds and in gyms. With individual games being only 10 minutes long and a fast-paced style, this event is designed to appeal to both die-hard basketball fans and casual viewers. Team USA beat France 17-10 in their first game of the women’s pool round. There will be three more days of pool rounds before the finals on July 28 that will determine the first-ever 3X3 Olympic champions.
