policies FAQs Q: I have odd tile size, what sticker size should I choose? We do custom design, custom fit to any odd, irregular shape tile or wall. I have more designs than you see in the store and if you don't see anything in store that you like, message us and we will hunt it down for you! Q: Can I use them on painted dry wall? Yes, be cautious when the wall is newly painted, do allow the paint to completely aired and cured. Ideally should wait for 3-4months before applying the stickers. The product is tested on most paint and should not damage the paint, however paint that are old and powdery or flaky may cause the paint to lift up when removing sticker. Q. What is a tile sticker? Self-adhesive vinyl film with thickness of less than 1mm. We use the most cutting edge printing technology added with lamination that seal all colour within the films to make it super durable and scratch resistant. Q: Are they waterproof and how durable are they? They are commercial grade heavy duty material that used for outdoor signage, vehicle wrap. Though they are relatively thin for ease of installation, they are all weather proof and suitable for wet area and outdoor application. We use UV outdoor color printing that guarantee no fading for atleast 7years outdoor or much longer if used indoor. Though the material is resistant however it is important that the surface must be non-porous and smooth for the adhesive to have full contact on the surface to avoid peeling over time Q: Can I use them in wet area like shower stall or wet toilet? YES. They are fully waterproof as long as the surface is smooth for the adhesive to work well. The surface should not be porous Q: Can I use the 24x48in big sheet over my existing tiles? We only recommend individually cut stickers to use on exiting tiled area, the sticker should NOT cover the existing grout-lines. However if the area is non-tiled, you can use either the Roll(24x48in) or square stickers. The Roll will have faux grout-lines on them so it looks like a grounted tiles. We also offer to add faux-grout on individually cut square sticker upon request. Q: Can I use it over lenoleum floor with existing tile pattern? What sticker size should I use? Yes. If the linoleum is completely flat without any uneven grout lines, you can choose any sticker size to cover up the entire linoleum without having to match the linoleum tile size. For such situation, we will custom make the stickers to include a FAUX GROUT-LINES printed around each stickers. Contact us for detail about Faux grout-line on stickers. What is Faux Grout? For flat surface without existing tile or grouts like drywall,flat linoleum vinyl floor, we can print faux grout around each sticker to make it look like grouted tile.
